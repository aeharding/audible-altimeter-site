The Open Audible

By Alexander Harding

Open Audible rev.5

An open source, hackable, $50 audible altimeter for skydivers.

Back on Kickstarter

Audible altimeters can go for $200+, and often have interfaces that are confusing and unnecessary. This audible is different:

  • Safety first
  • 100% open source and hackable
  • Always on operation, with an estimated one year battery life over 500 skydives. Set and forget!
  • Four different modes, with infinite customization when you hook it up to your computer
  • Low battery alarm when in the plane at 500 feet, and right after landing
  • No annoying LCD

Partlist

  • Atmel Atmega328P microprocessor running at 1Mhz
  • Bosch BMP180 barometer
  • TDK 4kHz, 3 Vo-op PS1240P02BT piezo speaker
  • 2 position DIP switch
  • FT232RL USB to serial UART interface
  • MicroUSB port
  • 2x 20mm CR2032 coin cell battery holders
  • Various 0805 resistors and caps, diode
  • 2x3 ISP header
